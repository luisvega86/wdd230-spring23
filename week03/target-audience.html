Target Audience

Our target audience consists of ambitious entrepreneurs, business owners, and professionals in Mar del Plata who are driven to succeed and contribute to the local economy. They actively seek resources, support, and networking opportunities to enhance their businesses. Whether they are established enterprises or startups, they are eager to overcome challenges, explore growth opportunities, and stay up to date with industry trends. They value collaboration, community engagement, and believe in the power of collective action to drive positive change. Our target audience includes government representatives invested in economic development, recognizing the importance of a strong business community for Mar del Plata's prosperity.


Personas

Business Owner: This persona represents entrepreneurs and business owners in Mar del Plata who are looking for resources, support, and networking opportunities. They visit the website to learn about membership benefits, access business resources, stay updated on upcoming events, and connect with other like-minded individuals to grow their businesses.


Community Member: This persona represents residents of Mar del Plata who are interested in the local business scene and want to stay informed about events, initiatives, and developments in the area. They visit the website to explore upcoming events, access resources for entrepreneurs, and learn about the economic impact of local businesses on the community.


Scenarios

  • Can I find information about the price of polo shirts in the chamber's Business Directory?
  • Which member banks offer online fund transfer services?
  • Can I find information about local sports events and their outcomes on the chamber's website?
  • Is there a section on the website dedicated to outdoor activities and weather updates for local beaches?
