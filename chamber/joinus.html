Membership Level Membership Fee Benefits
NP Membership No fee
  • Access to networking events
  • Listing in the chamber's online directory
  • Opportunity to participate in chamber committees and initiatives
  • Access to chamber resources and publications
Bronze Membership Annual fee applies
  • All benefits of NP Membership
  • Enhanced listing in the chamber's online directory
  • Invitation to exclusive member-only events
  • Opportunity to promote company news and updates through the chamber's communication channels
  • Access to discounted advertising options
Silver Membership Annual fee applies (higher than Bronze)
  • All benefits of Bronze Membership
  • Priority access to chamber training and workshops
  • Featured spotlights on the chamber's homepage or newsletter
  • Exclusive sponsorship opportunities for chamber events
  • Access to market research reports and industry insights
Gold Membership Annual fee applies (higher than Silver)
  • All benefits of Silver Membership
  • Complimentary attendance to chamber networking events and seminars
  • VIP access to high-profile chamber events
  • Dedicated account manager for personalized support
  • Prominent logo placement on the chamber's website and promotional materials
  • Priority consideration for leadership positions within the chamber
